## 🔧 How It Works

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile** (< 768px): Hamburger menu, cards, fullscreen drawer
- **Tablet** (768-1024px): Hybrid layout, better sizing
- **Desktop** (> 1024px): Sidebar, table view, side drawer

### Touch Optimization
- All buttons: 48×48px minimum (easy to tap with thumb)
- Forms: 16px font (no iOS zoom)
- Safe areas: Handles notches on new phones
- Dynamic height: Works with mobile address bar

### Animations
- Drawer: Smooth slide-in (0.3s)
- Menu: Fade and slide effects
- Buttons: Hover & active states
- Overall: Professional polish
